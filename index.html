About us

A Step To Better Health.

Psychotherapy is a meaningful, thoughtful and engaging conversation about your life.
Our Team

Lakeshore Psychotherapy Group, LLC. is a psychotherapy practice specializing in psychotherapy and assessments for adolescents, adults and couples. We believe that caring for your mental heath is vital to living a healthy life. We feel that psychotherapy should be accessible to everyone and we do our part in making that happen.

At LPG, our clients are more than a collection of symptoms. They are unique individuals with complex needs, convicts, wishes and struggles.

Our therapists are trained to understand the complexities of the human personality and how it is shaped by biology, early developmental experiences, and life events. We will help you understand the many factors that influence your thoughts, feelings, and behaviors and help you work through feelings and experiences that cause you pain and anxiety.
