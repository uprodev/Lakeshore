Services & Assessments

Individual, Couples, and Supervision and Consultation. Our practice aims to make significant positive changes in your life by providing needed support, a safe place to explore your thoughts and feelings, and valuable insights into your experiences.

Our goal is to share the emotional resources you need to grow and create a more joyful and loving life.

Psychotherapy

We Offer

Individual Therapy

Everyone experiences feelings of sadness, anger, resentment, negative self-talk, uncertainty and just feeling stuck at times. However, some of us have the tools to move through our emotions quickly. Others of us need some help in bringing our goals back into alignment, simply focusing, and re-balancing our energy.

Couples Therapy

Relationships constantly need attention. Couples who choose therapy does not mean that your relationship is in danger or even headed for divorce (or a breakup). Life serves us with issues and conflicts all of the time. Couples therapy is highly recommended during times of transition (i.e. moving in together, getting married, starting a family).

Adolescent Therapy

Psychotherapy can help your adolescent understand and manage his/her feelings more effectively. It can help your teen recognize and change ineffective coping strategies and re-examine negative beliefs about himself and others.

Child Therapy

You’ve noticed that your child is having problems. He/she may be feeling depressed, anxious and/or lonely but you’re not sure how to help. Maybe you thought your child was going through a “phase” and would grow out of it with time but it isn’t getting any better.
