Payment and Insurance

Payment Info and benefits

Payment Information

Dr. Danielle Brodsky and Diana C. Sanabria, LCSW are in-network with Blue Cross Blue Shield & Blue Choice insurance PPO plans. For those clients who have BCBS, we will verify your benefits and eligibility prior to the first appointment. We will also submit claim to BCBS on your behalf.

Out of Network benefits

Using out of networks benefits is simple. All you have to do is pay the fee directly at the time of service and we will provide you with a monthly “superbill” to submit to your insurance provider for reimbursement. The insurance company will pay you directly. Please call your insurance provider for more information about coverage for our services.

Find out your Out-of-Network Benefits

  • Call the customer service phone number on the back of your insurance card.

  • Tell the representative that you would like to know your out-of-network benefits for individual psychotherapy (90837) or couples therapy (90847).

  • Ask how much you will be reimbursed for each session.

  • Ask how and where to submit the “superbill” from your provider for reimbursement.
