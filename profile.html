About

Jeffrey A. Seiden, Psy.D.

Psychotherapy can be a powerful experience, helping you to understand yourself and grow emotionally. We often carry around with us the split off, unintegrated and unremembered pains and disappointments of our lives. These painful experiences typically cleave their way into our personalities in unintended ways that we don’t even realize. They can affect how we feel about ourselves, how we relate to others and how we act in the world. Psychotherapy is process of self discovery, a way of reclaiming our painful experiences and making use of them to propel our lives forward in constructive and meaningful ways. Insight and understanding frees us from the prison of the past and creates potential for new growth and experience in the present.

For over 20 years, I have worked with individuals and couples to increase their capacity to have meaningful and satisfying lives.
