What To Expect

When you’ve read all the books, heeded advice from your loved ones, and tried to make changes on your own, but you still don’t feel like yourself, it’s time to try a new approach. Sometimes, the challenges we face can’t be solved on our own; sometimes, we need to take a deeper look to find the root of the issue. From there, we can create real and lasting change.

Psychotherapy

We can help with

  1. Anxiety

    Constant worry, irritability, self-consciousness, hypochondriasis, feeling overwhelmed, insomnia.

  2. Depression

    Low self-esteem, and self-criticism, hopelessness, helplessness, negativity, lack of interest, shame and guilt.

  3. Eating Disorders

    They result in anxiety, low self-esteem, feelings of helplessness and loss of control, shame and intense self criticism.

  4. Loss and Mourning

    Therapy for loss of a loved one, divorce/separation, and illness.
